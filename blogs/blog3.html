Say Goodbye to Account Hassles

OpenAI is breaking barriers by making its flagship conversational AI ChatGPT accessible to everyone, regardless of whether they have created an account. However, while this move enhances accessibility, the experience may differ slightly. Importantly, all interactions will continue to contribute to the AI's training data unless users actively choose to opt out.

Starting today, in select markets, and gradually expanding worldwide, visitors to chat.openai.com will no longer encounter a login prompt. Instead, users will be seamlessly immersed in conversation with ChatGPT, utilizing the same advanced model as logged-in users. This simplification aims to streamline the user experience and encourage broader engagement with AI technology.

While users can freely engage in conversation, it's essential to note that certain features typically associated with persistent accounts, such as saving or sharing chats and utilizing custom instructions, may not be available to those without accounts. Nevertheless, OpenAI emphasizes the option for users to opt out of having their chats contribute to the AI's training data, providing a level of control and transparency in the interaction process.

You still have the option to opt out of your chats being used for training (which, one suspects, undermines the entire reason the company is doing this in the first place).
